Gospel for Asia

Gospel for Asia seeks to fulfill the great commission by sending national missionaries where the Gospel has never been preached. There are approximately 2 billion people in Asia who are unreached by the gospel. Gospel for Asia seeks to reach these lost souls by training and sending out national missionaries. These national missionaries can go where we can't and preach the gospel to those who have never heard it before. Learn more
